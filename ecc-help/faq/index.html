B: Datfile import/export

1: What is a Datfile?
A datfile contains meta-data, describing the rom-file. In a datfile the information, how the original filename is written, what kind of game the rom is and many more!
2: What datfiles could be imported!
You can import either emuControlCenter datfiles, including all meta-informations known by emuControlCenter or Romcenter-Datfiles, including basic meta-informations of the roms.
