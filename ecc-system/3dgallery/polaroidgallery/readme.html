Instructions

Add your pictures to photos.xml and place the photos.xml and polaroid.swf file together with all your pictures in your website folder. Then you can just place polaroid.swf in your html code and you're ready to go.

You can pass along the variable "xmlURL" if you wish to load a different xml-file than the default "photos.xml"

If you want lo load pictures from your flickr account you can pass along the variable "flickrID". If you don't know your flickr id, go to http://idgettr.com/ and find out.

You can also make the app only get pictures with spesific tags by sending the parameter flickrTAGS when embedding the swf
